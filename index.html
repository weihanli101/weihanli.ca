About Me

I am an undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Guelph expecting to finish in 2020.

I am intrigued by new ideas in the field of computer science, developing software in teams and on my own, and finding solutions to practical problems with programming. I mainly work in C and Java, and have also used Python, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. I have experience with front end responsive web development using Bootstrap. I have also developed android applications using the OpenGL framework LibGDX.

Besides programming I occupy the rest of my time with playing music, hiking, traveling, running, and snowboarding.

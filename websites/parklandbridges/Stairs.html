Stairs, Catwalks and Accessories

Parkland Bridges offers a wide range of complimentary fabricated products such as Bridge Canopies, Stairs and Railings to complete your bridge project or your industrial plant upgrade.

We have a comprehensive range of equipment, a spacious fabrication shop and the experienced designers, craftsmen and welders to master our customers' unique projects and to make this fabrication facility the "One Stop" shop to go to. We consider pedestrian and worker safety and environmental safeguards as top priority with all of our staircase and fabrication designs. Our staircases, platforms and accessories are built to meet or exceed OHSA regulations and provincial building codes, as well as the high design standards each of our customers have come to expect.

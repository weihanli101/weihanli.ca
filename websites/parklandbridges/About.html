Design, Engineering & Drafting Services

Parkland Bridges employs and contracts the region's leading professionals in bridge design (Cad Design, 3D Modeling), drafting and structural engineering.

We provide Urban and Municipal Planners, Parks and Recreation Planners, Landscape Architects, Designers and General Contractors the forward thinking expertise required to achieve their environmental, ecological and aesthetic visions no matter the geological conditions.

All designs conform to Can/CSA S6-06 Canadian Highway Bridge Design Code, OPSS 906 Specifications and all other provincial and municipal guidelines and we'll meet or exceed your own safety and design standards.

Bridge Fabrication

  • Custom Fabrication: Mild Steel, Stainless Steel or Corrosion resistant self-weathering steel (350A, 350AT, A588, Corten)
  • CWB Certified Welding to W47.1 and W59
  • Pedestrian, snowmobile, all-terrain vehicle , maintenance vehicles & light traffic bridges
  • Thresholds, approach rails, safety rails, rub rails and handrails and wheelchair ramps
  • Decking: pressure treated finished or rough sawn softwood or hard wood, steel bar grating, engineered non-slip formed steel, galvanized pans for poured concrete or asphalt
  • Bridge and Catwalk Canopies and Ornamental Embellishments
  • Commercial blast cleaning, galvanizing, epoxy coatings and painting

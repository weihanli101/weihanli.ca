Our History

Resource Industrial Group Inc. (RIG) is a well-established Ontario based privately held company. From its Original modest foundation in 1996, RIG has evolved toady into a regional leader in the Southwestern Ontario specializing in millwrighting, process piping and custom fabrication. The PARKLAND BRIDGE division born 15 years ago, specializes in design, manufacturing, construction, installation and maintenance of bridges.

Over the years, RIG has earned a solid reputation in its field for providing industrial solutions to a number of industries, including Food, Automotive, Solar, Chemical, Aerospace, Extrusion and Mining. Its dedication to high quality, on time delivery, exceptional flexibility, innovative solutions and honest pricing has earned it a loyal and dedicated clientele.
